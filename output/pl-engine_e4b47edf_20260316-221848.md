### 🔍 Smart Detection

- **New line items**: Consulting ($25k) → WARNING
- **Removed line items**: Freight ($50k → $0) → CRITICAL  
- **Large variances**: Utilities (-30%) → CRITICAL
- **Moderate changes**: Materials (+15%) → WARNING
- **Small changes**: <10% + <$1k → filtered out
