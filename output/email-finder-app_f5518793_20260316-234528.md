## ✅ Monetization Implementation Complete!

I've successfully added a complete monetization system to emailfinder.buildkit.store with LemonSqueezy payments, Supabase tracking, and rate limiting. Here's what was implemented:

### 🎯 Features Delivered

1. **Updated Pricing Tiers**

   - Pro: $99/month, 500 lookups/day (was $29/mo)
